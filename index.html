Instructions:
  • Use LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys or A and D keys to control your paddle
  • When you see a Power Up spawn on your side you can move towards it to pick it up. Once picked up, it will show on top left
    Types of Power Ups:
  • Expand: Expands your paddle for certain time
  • Rambo: Paddle shoots bullets for some time
  • Hot Knife: Ball goes through all the bricks it hits
  • Fat Boy: Shoots back a nuke towards bricks that will blow up all the bricks in its blast radius
